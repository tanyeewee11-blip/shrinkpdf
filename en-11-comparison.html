Smallpdf vs ILovePDF vs ShrinkPDF — Best Free PDF Compressor 2026

Quick Overview

At a Glance
  • Smallpdf: Polished UI, 2 free tasks per day, paid plan for heavy use
  • ILovePDF: Feature-rich, daily limits on free tier, files processed on servers
  • ShrinkPDF: Truly unlimited free, browser-based, privacy-first

Pricing and Limits

Smallpdf charges around USD 9 per month for unlimited use. ILovePDF has a similar pricing model. Both use the freemium approach where the free tier has daily task caps and file size restrictions.

ShrinkPDF is completely free with no task limits or file size restrictions, supported by non-intrusive display advertisements.

Privacy and Security

Smallpdf and ILovePDF upload your files to their servers for processing. Files are typically deleted after a short period, but they do pass through external servers. If your document contains sensitive information, this is worth considering.

ShrinkPDF processes files 100% in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device, making it the most private option available.

Compression Quality

All three tools provide good compression results for typical office documents. For very large files or specialized compression needs, server-side tools may achieve slightly higher compression ratios due to more powerful server-side algorithms like Ghostscript.
