Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

🔒 Short version: ShrinkPDF processes your PDF files entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

ShrinkPDF does not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not require account registration or login
  • We do not ask for your name, email, or any personal details
  • We do not store or have access to any PDF files you compress

2. How Your Files Are Processed

All PDF compression happens locally in your web browser using JavaScript. Your file is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party server. Once you close or refresh the page, all file data is cleared from memory.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We may use standard analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This may include:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • General geographic location (country/city level)
  • Browser type and device type

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

4. Advertising

ShrinkPDF may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site or other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • Google Fonts – for typography
  • Cloudflare Pages – for website hosting
  • pdf-lib – an open-source JavaScript library for PDF processing (runs locally in your browser)

6. Children's Privacy

ShrinkPDF does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our About page for contact information.
