Why Avoid Installing PDF Apps?

  • Most PDF apps require a paid subscription for full compression features
  • Apps request unnecessary permissions including camera and contacts access
  • Apps consume valuable storage space on your device
  • Privacy risk — some apps upload your files to their servers

How to Compress PDF on Android

1

Open Chrome on Android

Open Google Chrome on your Android device and navigate to ShrinkPDF.com.

2

Tap Choose PDF File

Tap the upload button and select your PDF from Files, Google Drive, or Downloads.

3

Select Compression Level and Compress

Choose your preferred level and tap Compress PDF Now. Processing happens on your device.

4

Download the Result

Tap the download button. The compressed PDF is saved to your Downloads folder.

How to Compress PDF on iPhone

1

Open Safari on iPhone

Open Safari and navigate to ShrinkPDF.com. The site works perfectly in Safari on iOS.

2

Tap Choose PDF File

Tap upload and select your PDF from the Files app or iCloud Drive.

3

Compress and Save

After compression, tap download. The file is automatically saved to your Files app.

Frequently Asked Questions

No. You need an internet connection to load the ShrinkPDF website. But once loaded, the compression itself uses your device processor not the internet.
Which mobile browser works best with ShrinkPDF? ▼
Chrome on Android and Safari on iPhone work best. Most modern mobile browsers are fully compatible.

Yes. Open the PDF from WhatsApp, save it to Files or Downloads, then upload to ShrinkPDF from your browser.
