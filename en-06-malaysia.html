Compress PDF for Malaysian Government Portal Upload — LHDN, JPA, EPF (2026)

Malaysian government portals — LHDN for tax filing, JPA for scholarships, EPF for fund matters — all have strict PDF size limits. This guide covers the main portals and how to compress your documents for free.

Malaysian Government Portal File Size Limits

Upload Limits by Portal
  • LHDN e-Filing: 2MB per document
  • UPU / MyGovUC: 2MB per file
  • JPA Scholarship Portal: 2MB per document
  • EPF i-Akaun: 1-2MB per file
  • SPA Online (civil service): 2MB per document
  • MySejahtera: 2MB per upload

Most Common Documents That Are Too Large

  • Income tax forms and salary slips (EA Form)
  • Academic transcripts scanned in high resolution
  • Supporting letters from employers or school principals
  • Passport photos embedded in PDF format
  • Bank statements covering multiple months combined
