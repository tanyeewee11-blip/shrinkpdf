Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?
ShrinkPDF converts PDF pages to JPG and PNG. JPG is smaller, PNG is lossless.
Will the text in images be selectable?
No. Once converted to an image, text becomes part of the image and cannot be selected or copied.
Is there a page limit for conversion?
No. Convert PDFs of any length with no page or file size limits.
Can I convert only specific pages?
Currently the tool converts all pages. Use the Split tool first to extract the pages you want before converting.
