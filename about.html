About ShrinkPDF

A free, privacy-first PDF compression tool built for everyday people — students, job seekers, office workers, and anyone who's ever been told their file is too large.

🎯 Why We Built This

PDF file size limits are frustrating. Whether you're uploading documents to LHDN, submitting a university application, sending a resume, or just trying to share a file on WhatsApp — you've probably hit a wall with "file too large".

Most free PDF compressors online either have strict file size limits (10–20MB), require you to create an account, show annoying watermarks, or upload your sensitive documents to their servers.

ShrinkPDF does none of that. Your files are compressed entirely in your browser — no uploads, no accounts, no limits, no watermarks. Always free.

⚙️ How It Works

ShrinkPDF uses pdf-lib, an open-source JavaScript library, to process your PDF directly in your browser. When you upload a file, it is read into your browser's memory — never sent over the internet.

The compression removes redundant data, optimises internal structures, and (depending on your chosen level) reduces image quality slightly to achieve smaller file sizes. The result is a new PDF file that you can download immediately.

Once you close the page or compress another file, all data is cleared from memory automatically.
