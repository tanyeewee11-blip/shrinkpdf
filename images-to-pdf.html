Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert to PDF?
ShrinkPDF supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP images. You can mix different formats in the same PDF.
Is there a limit to how many images I can combine?
No. Add as many images as you need — no count or file size limits apply.
Will my images lose quality when converted?
Images are embedded at their original resolution. Use Light compression afterwards if you need a smaller file.
Can I set a custom page size?
Pages are sized to match each image's dimensions. For A4 or letter-sized pages, resize images to match those proportions first.
